04 — Signature

the HUD language

Eight recurring details do most of the identity work. Use them consistently and any new screen will belong.

08 · Vertical fractal glass
reeded refraction
Fine vertical ribbing over a fluid spectral gradient, finished with grain. The poster-tier surface — splash backdrops, section headers and loading states.
01 · Chromatic hairline

A pink→purple→cyan gradient line, inset 18px, sits on the top edge of every panel. The single most repeated motif.

02 · HUD corner ticks

Purple top-left, cyan bottom-right L-brackets frame key panels and the splash screen.

03 · Chromatic aberration

Display headers get an RGB-split shadow — magenta left, cyan right, purple bloom.

nova
04 · Shimmer underline

A looping spectrum bar underlines the page title — motion without noise.

05 · Dot grid + spectral bloom

A 26px dot lattice and soft blurred color blooms give the void depth behind glass.

06+07 · Holo disc mark + grain

The spinning holographic vinyl is the brand mark at every scale; a fine grain overlay unifies the whole surface.
